## About the AI PREP4WORK Initiative

The **AI PREP4WORK Initiative** at Hillsborough College prepares students
across seven Associate of Science degree programs for careers that integrate
artificial intelligence tools and workflows. Funded programs include:

- Cybersecurity
- Cloud Computing
- Construction Management
- Architecture
- Accounting
- Business Administration
- Electrical and Electronics Technology (EET)

The K1 Wizard-of-Oz Dashboard is a curriculum technology deliverable of
this grant, designed for free reuse and adaptation by other postsecondary
institutions.
