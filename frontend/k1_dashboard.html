🚨 STOP — DO NOT TOUCH THE ROBOT YET
If K1 is in Walk mode and you touch or move it, it may react and injure you.
  1. Look at K1 from a safe distance. Is it standing or moving? Do NOT approach.
  2. Do NOT touch the robot until it is completely still.
  3. Locate the power button. Power off from a safe distance without grabbing the robot.
  4. Once powered off, carefully lay K1 face-down on the floor.
  5. Power back on. Wait for the startup tone and green indicator light.
  6. Robot will start in Damp mode — motors relaxed, lying on the floor. Safe to proceed.
  7. Return here and select "Lying face-down on the floor (Damp)".
🚨 If the robot is moving and you cannot safely reach the power button — call for assistance immediately. Do not attempt to stop it manually.
🚨 Emergency Stop
⚠ Always ensure a clear area around K1 before movement. If robot state is uncertain — use Emergency Stop, then power off before approaching.
